About the OECS PEARL School Grants

Welcome to the OECS PEARL School Leadership Grants Website! Our mission is to support school leaders in the implementation of innovative, change embedded projects geared towards improving the quality of education for all students, teachers, and relevant stakeholders.

This initiative is brought to you by the Organisation of the Eastern Caribbean States (OECS) Commission who has received funding from the Global Partnership for Education (GPE) for to support the implementation of OECS Education Sector Strategy (OESS). The Programme for Educational Advancement and Relevant Learning (PEARL) is a four-year programme that seeks to advance progress towards attaining the goal of the OESS through increased access and improved student learning in basic education.

Guided by the vision of the OESS: Every Learner Succeeds, component four the OECS PEARL aims to enhance educational leadership and management competencies and processes for improved classroom instruction and student achievement. Thus, our grant initiative is aimed at supporting the advancement of education in the region by facilitating the implementation of Continuous School and System Improvement plans. To achieve this, we prioritize supporting school leaders in developing innovative, evidence-based projects that can create sustainable change in the education sector. Please note that our grants are awarded for a one-year period, during which grantees will implement their projects and at the end thereof, will submit a research report detailing their project outcomes and impacts. This research report will be published for knowledge sharing purposes.
